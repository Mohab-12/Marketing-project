1.a) Which campaign is performing the best? Based on which criteria are you making such conclusions?

C1 is the best performing campaign because it has the highest IR which means that it has the lowest number of clicks that lead to an installation. Hence, it is the most effective campaign.

1.b) How would you suggest to optimise these campaigns or what changes would you recommend? Which campaign would you allocate more resources to?

To optimise these campaigns, the number of clicks and impressions have to increase by enhancing the quality of the advertisement. Basically, most of the campaigns has high IR. Thus, if the clicks are getting better, the installation will increase.

I would allocate more resources to C23 because it has high CTR, but low installations. In short, this campaign is promising because many people click on it, with some modifications it can be more downloadable.

1.c) What other conclusion can you make from the data?

Almost half of the campaigns got got impressions lower the median value which is the center point of al values.

Quite barely, all campaigns which got above average impressions are clickable and their number of clicks are above average
